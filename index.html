Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health?

▼

Mental health refers to your emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how you think, feel, and act in different situations.

How can I improve my mental health?

▼

Improving your mental health can include activities like exercise, meditation, talking to a therapist, or developing strong social connections.

When should I seek professional help?

▼

You should seek professional help when you feel overwhelmed, unable to cope, or if you're experiencing severe mood swings or thoughts of harm.

What are common signs of mental health issues?

▼

Common signs include changes in sleep patterns, feelings of hopelessness, isolation, and difficulty in focusing or managing daily tasks.

What resources are available for mental health support?

▼

Resources include therapy, online support groups, mental health hotlines, self-care tools, and various apps focused on mindfulness and mental health.
